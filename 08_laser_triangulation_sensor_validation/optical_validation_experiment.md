# Example Experiment: Laser Triangulation Sensor Validation

## Objective

Evaluate the accuracy and repeatability of a laser triangulation distance sensor.

## Setup

Components:

- semiconductor laser emitter
- optical lens system
- position-sensitive detector (PSD)
- analog signal processing electronics
- precision linear stage

## Method

A target was moved incrementally across the measurement range while recording PSD output voltage.

The resulting signal was analysed to determine:

- linearity of distance response
- signal noise characteristics
- repeatability across multiple measurements

## Outcome

The experiments produced characteristic curves defining sensor performance across the operating range, supporting calibration and product validation.
